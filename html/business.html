Small and Medium scale businesses

The SMEs processors in Kenya do not have adequate access to credit. There is a big niche for financial service providers and donors to address this anomaly. Opportunities to set up training centers on how to develop business plantations that meet financial requirements. Use of obsolete technology also provides gap for innovation and investments to upgrade the SMEs.
